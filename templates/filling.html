Teeth Filling Service

Description

Teeth filling is a common dental procedure that involves filling a cavity or hole in a tooth with a special material, such as composite resin or silver amalgam. This helps prevent further decay and damage to the tooth, and restores its shape and function. The procedure typically involves removing the decayed portion of the tooth, cleaning the area, and filling the cavity with the chosen material.
