Teeth Cleaning Service

Description

Teeth cleaning is a routine dental hygiene service that removes plaque and tartar buildup from the teeth and gums, and helps prevent tooth decay and gum disease. A dental cleaning involves a thorough examination of the mouth and teeth, followed by the use of special instruments to remove the buildup of plaque and tartar. This is typically followed by polishing the teeth to remove surface stains and improve their appearance.
