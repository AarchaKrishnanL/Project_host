Dental Crowning Service

Description

Dental crowns are a common restorative dentistry treatment that can improve the appearance and function of damaged or decayed teeth. A crown is a tooth-shaped cap that is placed over a damaged tooth to restore its shape, size, and strength. It can also be used to cover a dental implant or hold a bridge in place. Crowns are made of various materials, including porcelain, metal, and ceramic, and can be customized to match the color and shape of your natural teeth.
